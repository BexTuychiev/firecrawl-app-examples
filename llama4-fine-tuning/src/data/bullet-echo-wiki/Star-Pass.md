‚Å† ‚ú™ Star Pass is a Battle Pass system in Bullet Echo.

‚òÜ Generally a new hero is introduced in it and it costs real money.

‚≠ê The pass approximately is active for 90-120 days. (3 or 4 months)

üåü There are 3 Episode of a Star Pass. Each one is nearly 1 month (30 days). With each episode unlock there are more rewards to unlock for.

‚ú® Free rewards are available too. In tiers, free and Star Pass vs Star Pass Plus rewards is separate.

üÜï Star Pass is redesigned in 28 November 2024 Update. There are now free tiers and paid tiers separate.
