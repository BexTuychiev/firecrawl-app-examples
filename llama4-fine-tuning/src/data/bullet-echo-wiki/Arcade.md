# Arcade

[Sign in to edit](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fbullet-echo.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FArcade%3Fveaction%3Dedit&uselang=en)

- [Purge](https://bullet-echo.fandom.com/wiki/Arcade?action=purge)
- [Talk (0)](https://bullet-echo.fandom.com/wiki/Talk:Arcade?action=edit&redlink=1)

Arcade is a game mode in Bullet Echo which has a limited time. (3 days active only)

[![Arcadee ee](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/bullet-echo/images/7/76/Arcadee_ee.png/revision/latest?cb=20240907130954)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/bullet-echo/images/7/76/Arcadee_ee.png/revision/latest?cb=20240907130954)

Entry costs 1 ticket, regular or VIP.

[![Arcadeg](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/bullet-echo/images/8/81/Arcadeg.png/revision/latest?cb=20240907130955)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/bullet-echo/images/8/81/Arcadeg.png/revision/latest?cb=20240907130955)

VIP Tickets don't expire and doesn't have number limit that you can have.

Normal tickets expire when current Arcade ends and you can have 10 maximum regular tickets.

Regular Tickets can be earned through battles with winning.

VIP Tickets can't be earned, they must be bought with bucks.

At the end of Arcade road there's a skin reward. It's usually rare, could be epic skin too.

Rules change every new Arcade beginning.
