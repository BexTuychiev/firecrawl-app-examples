# Roulette

[Sign in to edit](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fbullet-echo.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FRoulette%3Fveaction%3Dedit&uselang=en)

- [History](https://bullet-echo.fandom.com/wiki/Roulette?action=history)
- [Purge](https://bullet-echo.fandom.com/wiki/Roulette?action=purge)
- [Talk (0)](https://bullet-echo.fandom.com/wiki/Talk:Roulette?action=edit&redlink=1)

Roulette is gacha reward mechanic. There is a small percentage of a reward you can get. Some roulettes are in [Arcade](https://bullet-echo.fandom.com/wiki/Arcade "Arcade"), [Shop](https://bullet-echo.fandom.com/wiki/Shop "Shop") etc. Cost is sometimes bucks, arcade token, or crypto chips.
